Bus Waiting Time Comparison

This visualization compares bus waiting time of M100 and M101 buses collected at their 27 common stops.

A two-sample t-test assuming unequal variances is used to test the null hypothesis that the wait time for M100 bus and that for M101 bus are the same. The calculated p-value is 0.015. Because it is less than the chosen level of significance 0.05, the null hypothesis is rejected.

Click a marker on the map to see the distribution of the waiting times and the average waiting time at that specific bus stop for M100 and M101 buses.
